Privacy Policy for Skywise

Last Updated: 2/6/24

1. Introduction
Skywise, a weather application developed by Modrn Magic LLC, is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and sharing of your information when you use the Skywise app.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Location Data: To provide localized weather information, Skywise collects and uses your location data.
Firebase Crashlytics: For improving app stability and performance, we use Firebase Crashlytics which may collect data pertaining to app crashes.
ChatGPT API: Skywise utilizes ChatGPT APIs for certain functionalities within the app.
Purpose: The collected data is exclusively used to deliver accurate weather forecasts and ensure smooth app functionality.
3. Data Sharing
Skywise does not share your personal data with any third parties. However, data may be processed by third-party services such as Firebase Crashlytics and ChatGPT APIs under their respective privacy policies.

4. Data Security
We employ standard data security practices to protect your information. As we primarily collect non-critical data like email addresses, we strive to ensure their security through our partnership with Firebase.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Retention: User data is retained indefinitely as we currently do not have a system for automatic deletion.
Deletion Requests: Users can request the deletion of their email address by contacting support@modrnmagic.app or filling out this form: https://forms.gle/LKrGRKfWGJFw8c5p9.

6. User Rights and Controls
Users have the right to request the deletion of their email address from our database by contacting us at support@modrnmagic.app.

7. Compliance
Skywise is intended for use exclusively within the United States and does not claim compliance with GDPR, CCPA, or other specific data protection regulations.

8. Contact Information
For any inquiries related to privacy or data protection, please contact support@modrnmagic.app.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
Users will be informed of any changes to this Privacy Policy through updates within the Skywise app or via a notification to the email address provided by the user.
